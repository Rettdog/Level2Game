Ninjoreo

by: Garrett Kunkler

This game was made for my level 2 project at The League of Amazing Programmers.
Click here to download and play!

1. Why I Made This Game

Ninjoreo was a character I created in elementary school and I thought it would be cool to make a game about him. It was original a plat-former on Scratch and instead I made it a vertical scroller. After that, I thought it would be cool to make different characters that were also snack food warriors, and that is how Ninjoreo and his friends were born.

2. What I Learned

I realized that I needed better instructions because the beginning of the game is the hardest part, and some people quit before they can even start playing. I added better instructions and added more versatile controls so that more people could have fun playing it.

3. Architecture of My Game

There is one main GamePanel class that runs everything overall game related, while ObjectManager manages all of the platforms, background, enemies and some parts of the character. There are also other small classes that run certain aspects of the game such as MusicPlayer and ThemeButton. GamePanel calls most of the methods for these classes and makes sure that the game looks right, by calling all of the update and draw methods.

4. Technical Implementation

The MusicPlayer was difficult to time because it is hard to find out of music is playing or not. I made 4 nested switch statements for all of the different music that called the looping music method. This allowed me to loop the sound tracks over and over again. I also made a stopSound method that I called whenever the song was supposed to change. GamePanel was constantly calling playMusic but it would only work if sound wasn't playing.

5. Most Challenging Part

Implementing two types of enemies was the hardest because it caused a lot of other errors in other parts of the game. I had to recode all of the checks for when you collide with an enemy because it would only count it half of the time. Music Composition and recording was also difficult.

6. Future Fixes and Updates

MORE SKINS!!!!!!!!! Making it run on a phone as an app would also be better for sharing with friends. A pause button would be nice so that I could take actual screenshots.
